Core Features

Less, but everything that matters

Engineered for low latency, zero distraction, and absolute reliability — one job, done well.

⌨️

Any key to any click

Bind any keyboard shortcut — including free combinations of ⌘ ⇧ ⌥ ⌃ — or a mouse side button to an exact coordinate inside the target window. Press it, get a click in under 5 ms. Faster than your hand. More precise than your aim.

🖱️

The cursor never moves

Clicks are dispatched directly to the target. Your pointer stays exactly where you left it — nothing in flight is interrupted.

📱

Made for iOS games on Mac

Native AppKit apps, Chromium browsers, and iPhone or iPad games on Apple Silicon — all handled automatically. Resize the window and your mappings scale right along with it.

🎬

Multi-step macros, one trigger

A single keypress can fire a sequence of clicks, each with its own delay — seconds or minutes — and looped a fixed number of times or indefinitely. Press the trigger again to stop instantly. The menu bar glows red while it runs. Daily quests, periodic harvests, repeated refreshes: handled while you do something else.

🔇

Smart enough to know when you're typing

When focus lands in a chat box or search field, mappings step quietly aside and your keystrokes pass through untouched. Leave the field and they snap right back. Switch between typing and triggering without ever thinking about it.

🛡️

Private. Offline. Always.

No telemetry. No network calls. No screen reading. Your config stays on your Mac — and the only permission needed is for keys and window geometry.
